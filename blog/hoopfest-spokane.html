Annual Events

Hoopfest — Spokane's Biggest Weekend

Every last weekend of June, downtown Spokane transforms into the basketball capital of the world. Hoopfest — the world's largest 3-on-3 basketball tournament — takes over 45 city blocks with 450 courts, 6,000+ teams, and over 225,000 spectators. If you haven't experienced it, you truly can't imagine the scale.

This isn't just a basketball tournament. It's Spokane's single biggest event of the year, and one of the most unique sporting events in the entire country.

What Is Hoopfest?

Founded in 1990, Hoopfest has grown from a local tournament into an internationally recognized event. For one weekend, the streets of downtown Spokane become an outdoor basketball arena. Courts are set up on every block, and games run from early morning until evening. There are divisions for every age and skill level — from kids just learning the game to elite players competing for serious prizes.

But Hoopfest is about more than basketball. The surrounding festival features live music on multiple stages, food vendors from across the region, merchandise booths, contests, and a party atmosphere that's infectious. Even if you don't play basketball, Hoopfest is an incredible spectator experience.

By the Numbers

  • 6,000+ teams
  • 25,000+ players
  • 225,000+ spectators
  • 450 courts across 45 city blocks
  • 3,000 volunteers
  • 35+ years of history

When & Where

Hoopfest 2026

When: Last weekend of June (typically Saturday–Sunday)

Where: Downtown Spokane — 45 city blocks

Distance from Manito Bungalow: ~3.5 miles (10-minute drive)

Registration: spokanehoopfest.net

Cost: Free to spectate; team registration fees vary

Why Stay at Manito Bungalow for Hoopfest

During Hoopfest weekend, downtown Spokane hotels book up months in advance and prices skyrocket. Manito Bungalow offers a smarter alternative: a beautiful home on the South Hill, just 10 minutes from downtown, that sleeps up to 10. Bring your whole team, spread out in a real house, and enjoy the kind of comfort that a hotel simply can't match.

After a long day on the courts (or in the stands), come home to a full kitchen, a washer and dryer, and a porch where you can relax. Take a morning walk through Manito Park to loosen up before games, or grab a post-tournament beer at Lumberbeard Brewing, just 5 minutes away.

Hoopfest weekend fills up fast. Book Manito Bungalow early to secure your team's home base.
