If you only visit one brewery during your Spokane trip, make it **No-Li Brewhouse**. Sitting right on the banks of the Spokane River, No-Li is the brewery that put Spokane craft beer on the national map — and the experience of sipping an award-winning IPA while watching the river flow by is one of the signature Spokane moments.

## The Beer

No-Li has been winning awards since they opened, and their lineup is a testament to Pacific Northwest brewing at its finest. A few standouts:

- **Born & Raised IPA** — The flagship. A perfectly balanced West Coast IPA that's become synonymous with Spokane craft beer. Citrusy, piney, and endlessly drinkable.
- **Wrecking Ball Imperial Stout** — When the weather turns cold (and it does in Spokane), this is your beer. Rich, smooth, and warming. A cold-weather classic.
- **Big Juicy IPA** — For haze lovers. Juicy, tropical, and packed with hop flavor without the bitterness.

The tap list rotates regularly with seasonals and limited releases, so there's always something new to try alongside the staples.

## The Patio

This is what makes No-Li truly special. The riverfront patio is one of the best outdoor dining experiences in Spokane. On a warm summer evening, with the Spokane River right below you and a fresh pint in hand, you'll understand why locals consider this a sacred spot. Get there early on weekends — it fills up.

## The Food

No-Li's food menu goes well beyond typical brewery fare. Expect creative pub food with quality ingredients that pair well with the beer. The kitchen takes the food as seriously as the brewers take the beer.
