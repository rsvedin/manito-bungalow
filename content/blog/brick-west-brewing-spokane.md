**Brick West Brewing** is the kind of place that feels like it was designed for a good time with a group. Located in downtown Spokane's west end, this brewery occupies a gorgeous industrial space and fills it with excellent craft beer, solid food, and an energy that's hard to beat on a Friday night.

## The Space

The first thing you notice at Brick West is the space itself. High ceilings, exposed brick, an open floor plan with plenty of room to spread out — it's industrial-chic without being cold. There's a big bar, communal tables, and enough seating that you can usually find a spot even on busy weekends. If you're visiting Spokane with a group (Manito Bungalow sleeps 10, after all), this is a great place to start the night.

## The Beer

Brick West's lineup spans the full range of craft beer styles. Operated by a team of service industry professionals who know their way around a taproom, the quality is consistently high. They're not afraid to experiment with seasonal and limited releases, and the rotating taps mean there's a reason to come back every time you're in Spokane.

## Events & Atmosphere

Brick West has one of the more active events calendars in Spokane's brewery scene. Live music, trivia nights, and themed events keep things lively throughout the week. If you're looking for something to do on a Tuesday or Wednesday evening, check their social media for what's happening.
