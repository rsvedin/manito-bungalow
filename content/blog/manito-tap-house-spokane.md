If you're staying at Manito Bungalow, **Manito Tap House** is your neighborhood gastropub — and it's a three-minute walk from the front door. That alone would be enough to recommend it, but the truth is, Manito Tap House would be a destination even if it weren't so absurdly close.

## The Taps

Fifty draft beers. Five-zero. And these aren't filler selections — Manito Tap House curates their tap list with a keen eye for quality, pulling from the best Pacific Northwest breweries alongside stellar national and international options. If you're trying to explore Spokane's beer scene without brewery-hopping, this is the place to start. One visit and you'll have sampled beers from half a dozen local breweries.

## The Food

This isn't your average pub menu. Manito Tap House is committed to sustainability and scratch-made cooking — their recipes use quality ingredients and the menu includes thoughtful gluten-friendly, vegetarian, and vegan options alongside hearty classics. The burger is legendary among South Hill locals, and for good reason.

The kitchen takes the food as seriously as the bar program, which is exactly what elevates Manito Tap House from "good pub" to "great gastropub."

## The Vibe

Industrial-chic meets neighborhood warmth. The space is well-designed — high ceilings, an open kitchen, a long bar — but it never feels pretentious. Families come for early dinners. Friends gather over flights. It's the kind of place where you can have a first date or a birthday party and feel equally at home.
