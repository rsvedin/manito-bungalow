We're thrilled to officially launch the Manito Bungalow website — your new home base for everything related to our beautifully restored 1910 craftsman bungalow on Spokane's beloved South Hill.

## A Home with History

Built in 1910, Manito Bungalow has been lovingly restored to preserve all the original character that makes craftsman homes so special — hardwood floors, high ceilings, and thoughtful details at every turn — while adding the modern comforts today's travelers expect.

## One Block from Manito Park

Our location is hard to beat. Step out the front door and you're a one-minute walk from Manito Park's 90 acres of rose gardens, Japanese garden, conservatory, and mirror pond. It's Spokane's crown jewel, and it's practically in our backyard.

## Perfect for Groups

With three bedrooms sleeping up to 10 guests, a fully stocked kitchen, washer and dryer, and a private outdoor space, Manito Bungalow is built for families, reunions, and friend groups who want more than a hotel room.
