Every May, Spokane fills with proud families, decorated mortarboards, and an unmistakable energy of celebration. With four major universities in the area, **graduation season** is one of the biggest travel weekends of the year — and finding the right place to stay for the whole family can be a challenge.

That's where [Manito Bungalow](/) comes in. With room for up to 10 guests across three bedrooms, a full kitchen for celebratory dinners, and a location that puts you close to every campus, it's the ideal home base for graduation weekend.

## 2026 Graduation Dates

### Gonzaga University — Class of 2026

Gonzaga's commencement celebrations take place over **Mother's Day Weekend, May 9–10, 2026**. Here's the full schedule:

**Saturday, May 9, 2026:**
- **Law Commencement:** 9:00–10:30 AM — McCarthey Athletic Center
- **Graduate Commencement:** 12:00–2:00 PM — McCarthey Athletic Center
- **Commencement Mass:** 2:45–4:00 PM — St. Aloysius Church

**Sunday, May 10, 2026 (Mother's Day):**
- **Undergraduate Commencement:** 9:30 AM–12:30 PM — Spokane Arena (six guest tickets per student)

**Location:** Gonzaga University Campus & Spokane Arena
**Distance from Manito Bungalow:** ~4 miles (12 minutes)
**Tickets:** Guest tickets required for all ceremonies, available to participating students starting mid-April. Undergraduate limit is 6 tickets. No limit for Law and Graduate ceremonies.
**Livestream:** All ceremonies are livestreamed at [gonzaga.edu/commencement](https://www.gonzaga.edu/commencement)
**Details:** [gonzaga.edu/commencement](https://www.gonzaga.edu/commencement)

Gonzaga's commencement weekend is one of the most well-attended in the region. The undergraduate ceremony moved to the larger Spokane Arena to accommodate nearly 2,000 graduates and their families. Expect campus-wide celebrations, family receptions, and packed restaurants across Spokane. The 2026 ceremony falls on Mother's Day weekend, making it an especially meaningful celebration.

**Pro tip:** Gonzaga families often arrive Thursday or Friday for pre-commencement events, departmental receptions, and the Baccalaureate Mass. Plan for a 3–4 night stay to enjoy the full experience.

### Whitworth University — Class of 2026

**Commencement:** Sunday, May 17, 2026 at 3:00 PM
**Location:** Whitworth Pine Bowl (outdoor amphitheater), Spokane
**Distance from Manito Bungalow:** ~8 miles (18 minutes)
**Details:** [whitworth.edu/commencement](https://www.whitworth.edu/commencement)

Whitworth's outdoor commencement in the Pine Bowl is one of the most picturesque graduation settings in the Northwest. The intimate ceremony reflects the university's close-knit community. Arrive early for good seating.

### Eastern Washington University — Class of 2026

**Commencement:** Saturday, June 13, 2026
**Location:** Roos Field, Cheney, WA
**Distance from Manito Bungalow:** ~18 miles (25 minutes)
**Details:** [ewu.edu/commencement](https://www.ewu.edu/commencement)

EWU holds its ceremony at Roos Field on the Cheney campus. The drive from Manito Bungalow is an easy 25 minutes on I-90. EWU's later June date means less competition for accommodations, but Spokane is still the best base for visiting families.

### WSU Health Sciences Spokane — Class of 2026

**Commencement:** Thursday, May 7, 2026
**Location:** The Fox Theater, Downtown Spokane
**Distance from Manito Bungalow:** ~3.5 miles (10 minutes)
**Details:** [spokane.wsu.edu/commencement](https://spokane.wsu.edu/commencement/)

WSU's Spokane campus graduates students from the College of Pharmacy, College of Nursing, and College of Medicine. The ceremony at the historic Fox Theater is an elegant and memorable event. Downtown dining options abound for a post-ceremony celebration.
