One of the best parts of staying on Spokane's South Hill is the dining. Here are the restaurants we send every guest to — all within easy reach of Manito Bungalow.

## Manito Tap House — 3 Minute Walk

An organic-certified, scratch-kitchen gastropub right past Manito Park on Grand Boulevard. The burger is legendary and the rotating craft beer tap list is genuinely exciting. This is the spot you'll visit more than once.

## Remedy Kitchen & Tavern — 5 Minute Walk

A true South Hill institution on Grand Boulevard. The menu is approachable, the vibe is warm, and it's the kind of place you end up going back to twice in one trip. Perfect for a relaxed dinner.

## Wooden City — 10 Minute Drive

One of Spokane's most talked-about restaurants. The menu is built on local ingredients with genuinely inventive dishes and service that knows what it's doing. Book ahead — it fills up fast.

## Perry Street Brewing — 8 Minute Drive

A beloved neighborhood brewery in the Perry District with excellent beer, good food, and a patio that fills up fast on warm evenings. Great for a casual afternoon.

## Two Seven Public House — 7 Minute Drive

A mellow South Hill pub with a loyal following. The tacos and burger are both worth ordering. Great when you want something low-key and satisfying.

## Inland Pacific Kitchen — 12 Minute Drive

Spokane's farm-to-table flagship. The menu changes with the seasons and every dish shows real intention. This is the special-occasion pick — and it delivers every time.
