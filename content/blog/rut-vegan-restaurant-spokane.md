Spokane isn't always the first city that comes to mind for plant-based dining — but **Rüt** is changing that perception one incredible dish at a time. As hosts at Manito Bungalow, we've sent dozens of guests here, and the feedback is always the same: "We can't believe that was vegan."

Whether you're fully plant-based, flexitarian, or just someone who appreciates creative, well-executed food, Rüt belongs on your Spokane dining list.

## The Food

Rüt's menu is inventive without being gimmicky. Every dish is 100% plant-based, but the focus is on flavor, texture, and presentation rather than trying to imitate meat. Think creative bowls, house-made sauces, fresh ingredients, and combinations that surprise you in the best way. The menu rotates seasonally, which means there's always something new to discover.

What makes Rüt special is the care that goes into every plate. This isn't "vegan food" as an afterthought — it's a restaurant that happens to be vegan, run by people who are passionate about proving that plant-based cooking can be the most exciting thing on your plate.

## The Atmosphere

The space is bright, welcoming, and has that effortless Pacific Northwest cool that Spokane does so well. It's casual enough for a weekday lunch but special enough for a date night. The staff is warm and knowledgeable about the menu, and they're always happy to make recommendations.

## Why We Love It

We're not vegan ourselves, but Rüt has become one of our go-to restaurants in Spokane. That says everything. When a restaurant can make committed omnivores choose it over a steakhouse, you know the food is doing something right. It's the kind of place that expands what you think is possible with plant-based cooking.
