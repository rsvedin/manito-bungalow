Walk five minutes south from Manito Bungalow along Grand Boulevard and you'll find **Remedy Kitchen & Tavern** — the South Hill's go-to spot for elevated pub fare, well-crafted cocktails, and a rooftop patio that's the best-kept secret in the neighborhood.

## The Food

Remedy calls it "elevated pub cuisine," and they mean it. The menu goes beyond the usual pub standards with thoughtful, well-executed dishes that use quality ingredients. It's the kind of food that surprises you for a neighborhood tavern — in the best way. The portions are generous, the flavors are dialed in, and there's enough range on the menu to satisfy everyone in your group.

## The Drinks

Twenty-four taps pour a rotating selection of craft beer, with a strong lean toward Pacific Northwest breweries. But what really sets Remedy apart from other pubs is the cocktail program. The craft cocktails are creative, well-balanced, and made with intention. If beer isn't your thing, you won't feel like an afterthought here.

## The Rooftop Patio

During Spokane's warmer months (which stretch longer than most people expect — May through October is beautiful), Remedy's rooftop patio is where you want to be. It's a genuine rooftop space with open-air seating that overlooks the South Hill's tree-lined streets. On a warm summer evening, it's perfect.

## The Banquet Hall

Visiting Spokane for a special occasion? Remedy has a full-service banquet hall for private events. Combined with the main dining room and bar, it's one of the more flexible spaces on the South Hill for groups. And with Manito Bungalow sleeping 10 just five minutes away, the logistics are easy.
